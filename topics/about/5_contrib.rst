Walter Rhein
------------

Walter Rhein maintains a blog about Peru at `StreetsOfLima.com <http://streetsoflima.com/>`__ ,
and contributes regularly to `Silent Sports Magazine <http://www.silentsports.net/>`__ . His
novels `The Reader of Acheron <https://www.amazon.com/dp/0991057341/>`__
and `Reckless Traveler <https://www.amazon.com/dp/0991465482/>`__ were
published with Perseid Press. His novel `The Bone Sword <https://www.amazon.com/dp/B00O6LOI8Y/>`__
was published by :ref:`harrenpress`. His novel, `Beyond Birkie Fever <https://www.amazon.com/dp/1492879347/>`__ , was originally
published by Rhemalda Press. He can be reached for questions or comments
at: WalterRhein@gmail.com.
